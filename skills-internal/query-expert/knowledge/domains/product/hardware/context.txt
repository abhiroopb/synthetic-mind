Device Types and Identifiers:
- reader_type: Hardware device types (e.g., Terminal, Register, Reader, Stand)
- reader_type_key: Specific hardware model versions
- reader_type_family: Higher level grouping of related reader types
- fatp_serial: Unique serial number for hardware devices (also referred to as: serial number, FATP, or unique identifier for a hardware/reader type). 
- location_id: Unique identifier for customer's physical location 
- customer_id: Unique identifier for the customer
- WOM: Week of Manufacture (derived from first 3 characters of FATP serial)

Device Details: 
- vertical or app_name: Point of Sale (POS) product used for the transaction or app
- app_version: point of sale (POS) version 
- os_name: Name of the operating system
- os_version: Full version number of the operating system 
- firmware version: the version of the firmware 

Data Update Frequency:
- Event tables are typically refreshed once per day
- All dimension tables are refreshed once per day 

Device Purchase and Distribution
- Shop purchases can be tracked through YOUR_HARDWARE_SCHEMA.FACT_SHOP_LINE_ITEM
- Third-party retail purchases (Amazon, Best Buy) aren't directly tracked. A device is sent to the retailer and sits on the shelf. We do not know about this device until the customer turns it on / takes a payment
- Can identify device usage through activation events in product_events

Important Metrics:
- Payment methods: dips, swipes, taps (counts and volume)
- Performance metrics: connectivity, transactions, errors, crashes 
- Firmware metrics: adoption rates, update success/failure
- Warranty and returns tracking
- Transaction success rates and error tracking

Two ways to measure active devices:
- Payment-based: Device took a payment
- Telemetry-based: Device sent heartbeat signals
