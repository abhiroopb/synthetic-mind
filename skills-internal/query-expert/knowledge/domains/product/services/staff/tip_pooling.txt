# Brand A Staff Tip Pooling - Data Guide

## Business Context

Tip Pooling is a Brand A Staff feature that allows merchants to distribute tips among employees using different methods beyond direct attribution. This feature is critical for Food & Beverage businesses where equitable tip distribution is a core operational need.

**Key Business Facts:**
- #1 product request for Shifts among F&B sellers
- Primary adoption in Food & Beverage businesses
- Part of Shifts Plus/Premium pricing tier
- Launched to "kill the tipping spreadsheet" for sellers
- Four distribution methods available (Direct, By Transaction, By Hours Worked, By Percentage)
- Percentage-based pooling launched in 2024 (go/YOUR_INTERNAL_LINK)

## Tip Distribution Methods

Brand A offers four types of tip distribution:

1. **Tip Directly (DIRECT):** Tips attributed to the team member who collected the tip
2. **Pool by Transaction (POOLED):** Tips split equally among all tip-eligible team members clocked in at time of transaction
3. **Pool by Hours Worked (POOLED_BY_HOURS):** Tips split based on hours worked
   - **Workday (POOLED_BY_HOURS_WORKDAY):** 24-hour workday period
   - **Workweek (POOLED_BY_HOURS_WORKWEEK):** 7-day workweek period
4. **Pool by Percentage (POOLED_BY_PERCENTAGE):** Tips split by user-defined percentage per job role
   - **Workday (POOLED_BY_PERCENTAGE_WORKDAY):** Daily percentage allocation
   - **Workweek (POOLED_BY_PERCENTAGE_WORKWEEK):** Weekly percentage allocation

## Primary Tables

### YOUR_ANALYTICS_SCHEMA.SQS_FEATURE_USAGE (VERIFIED)
**Purpose:** Primary table for tracking tip pooling adoption and activity
**Grain:** One row per merchant per feature per day
**Key for:** Historical tip pooling trends and active merchant counts

**Tip Pooling Feature Names:**
- `labor_tip_pooling_by_transaction` - Pool by transaction method
- `labor_tip_pooling_by_hours_workday` - Pool by hours (24-hour workday)
- `labor_tip_pooling_by_hours_workweek` - Pool by hours (7-day workweek)
- `labor_tip_pooling_by_percentage_workday` - Pool by percentage (daily)
- `labor_tip_pooling_by_percentage_workweek` - Pool by percentage (weekly)

Important Business Rules
Feature Usage Table is Primary: Always use YOUR_ANALYTICS_SCHEMA.SQS_FEATURE_USAGE for historical tip pooling trends and active merchant counts, not UNIT_TIP_DISTRIBUTION_SETTINGS

Tip Eligibility Required: Only employees marked as "tip eligible" in their profile receive pooled tips

Percentage Allocation Rules:

Percentages must add up to 100%
Allocated by job role, not individual employees
Employees with same job split their role's percentage by hours worked
Unattributed Tips: If no one is clocked in during a tip period, tips can be distributed equally among all tip-eligible employees who worked that workday (if enabled)

Time Period Definitions:

Workday: 24-hour period
Workweek: 7-day period
Legal Compliance: Tip pooling methods must comply with federal, state, and local laws. Brand A provides disclaimers but merchants are responsible for compliance

Pricing: Percentage-based pooling is part of Shifts Plus/Premium tier

Geographic Availability: Available in all markets except Japan (where tipping is not supported)

Key Metrics: 

Tip Pooling Attach Rate: % of eligible sellers using any tip pooling method
F&B Attach Rate: % of Food & Beverage sellers using tip pooling
Active Tip Pooling Sellers: Merchants with m_events > 0 in last 30 days
Pool Duration: Time between start_utc and end_utc of tip pool
Average Tip Rate: Total pool tips / pool duration hours
Employee Work Hours: Overlapping hours between employee shift and pool period
