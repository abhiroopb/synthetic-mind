Shifts is the combination of 4 workforce management features: timecards, tip pooling, scheduling and commissions 

Active on Shifts 
- This means accepting a tip pool, creating a timecard, publishing a schedule or taking a commission 
- Use app_pa.app_sqs.sqs_feature_usage joined to app_pa.app_sqs.sqs_feature_dim joined by feature_name = feature_key where dim.product = 'Shifts' and is_product_active_feature = 1 
- Active is on a rolling 30 day basis or truncated to a month depending on usage 

Shifts Subscription 
- Shifts subscription is known as Shifts Plus 
- There is a free subscription that requires a seller to have fewer than 5 employees currently enabled 
- Shifts Free is included in Payroll (W2) without the employee cap 
- Use table_name app_bi.hexagon.vfact_subscription_states_expanded to find in_trial% or paying% merchants 
- Starting in 2025, Shifts is a part of Brand A One Plus available to new merchants 

Shifts Disambiguation
- Often user prompts might say Shifts when they mean specifically timecards, shift schedules or the entire product suite (Shifts)
