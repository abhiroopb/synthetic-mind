# Cash App Growth Writing: Push Notifications and House Ads

## Push Notification: Headline
- First ~30 characters visible; up to 65 allowed — target ~30 chars
- No periods at end of single-sentence headlines
- Avoid starting with "Cash App" (notification already includes the logo)
- Front load action or value; avoid rhetorical questions that open the door to "no"

✅ "Make your money work for you" | "Save for goals, earn interest"
❌ "You can earn interest on savings when you have a Cash App Card" (too long, too detailed)
❌ "Cash App Taxes helps you get a max refund" (repeats product name)

## Push Notification: Body
- Up to 178 characters; use standard punctuation
- Put important parts early — visible on lock screen preview
- Focus on what matters most; lean on product/destination for details
- Don't write "tap to X" — the medium is inherently tappable
- If required by legal, can end with "Terms apply."

✅ "Save for your goals and earn interest to reach them faster—no fees, no minimum balance."
✅ "Spend up to $600 at Walmart and pay over 6 weeks. Shop now in Cash App."
❌ "Cash App now lets you earn interest on your savings..." (about us, not them)
❌ "Tap to order a Cash App Card by X/X..." (redundant "Tap to")

## House Ad: Headline
- Up to 40 characters (stays on one line)
- No periods at end of one-sentence headlines
- Clear, actionable; avoid rhetorical questions; focus on one or two things

✅ "Saving is better with direct deposit" | "Earn up to 4.5% interest"
❌ "Unlock more from Cash App" (too vague) | "Want to get benefits for saving?" (rhetorical)
❌ "Earn up to 4.5% interest when you direct deposit $300 in a month" (too long)

## House Ad: Body
- Ideally ≤90 characters (~3 lines); up to 125 (4 lines) if needed
- Use standard punctuation
- If no clear action in the title, put one in the body
- They're in the app — usually don't need to say "Cash App" (except branded terms)

✅ "Spend up to $600 at Walmart and pay over 6 weeks."
✅ "Save for your goals and make your money work for you—no fees, no minimum balance."
❌ "Save for your goals with Cash App and make your money work..." (redundant "Cash App")
❌ "Cash App Taxes has a max refund guarantee." (no action for the user)
