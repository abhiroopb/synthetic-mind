# Cash App Transactional Communications

Guidelines for email, push, and SMS covering money movement, account updates, and important customer information.

## Core Principles

**Speak like a human** — Conversational dialogue between Cash App and the customer. Read it out loud—if it sounds robotic, rewrite it.

**Lead with what matters** — Put primary information in headers and buttons (status or required action). Supporting context goes in body copy.

**Give context to move forward** — Make it clear why the message is relevant. When a transaction is unsuccessful or time-sensitive, explain how to course correct with a clear CTA.

**Don't direct blame or emotions** — Tell people what they need to know straightforwardly. Don't apologize for negative outcomes when the transaction was intentional. Avoid "congrats!" and "good news!" for outcomes that might not be positive for all customers (e.g., increased borrow limits).

**Make responsibility clear** — In negative outcomes (failure, declined), connect the outcome to the transaction type, not the customer's attempt.
- ✅ "Your transaction failed" ❌ "Your attempt to send money failed"

**Be radically consistent** — Maintain clean, consistent structure and tone. Never use ALL CAPS for urgency ("IMPORTANT" in subject lines); it signals illegitimate sources.

## Email

**Character limits**: Subject <30 chars / Pre-header <40 chars / Header <30 chars

✅ Do
- Put the most important info in the subject line; keep it short
- Use pre-header to elaborate on next steps ("Review all transaction details in the app")
- Personal greetings in Letter template only (not Notification or Receipt)
- Greeting format: "Hello [first_name]," — not "Hi", not first + last name
- Include a button when directing to a specific place in the app
- Be specific about app locations ("Visit the Activity tab in the app")
- Hyperlink descriptive text for URLs ("Review our terms of service")

❌ Don't
- Use vague time terms: "recent" or "just" ("Your recent transaction" / "Steve just requested $4")
- Dump URLs in body copy without descriptive hyperlinks
- Repeat "Cash App" in CTA text (channel already signals it's from Cash App)

---

## SMS

**Character limit**: Body <160 chars (without emojis)

✅ Do
- Lead with "Cash App:" for consistent sender identification
- Count "Cash App:" in the character total
- Be specific about app locations and actions

❌ Don't
- Include links/short URLs (trust and scam concerns)
- Use vague time terms ("recent", "just")
- Repeat "Cash App" in CTAs

---

## Push Notifications

**Character limits**: Header <30 chars / Body <160 chars (both required)

✅ Do
- Include both title and body copy (both required)
- Use deep links where possible
- Use header for both Android and iOS when possible
- Be specific about app locations when deep links aren't available

❌ Don't
- Use vague time terms ("recent", "just")
- Repeat "Cash App" in CTAs
