# Brand A Troubleshooting Article Template

## Principles
Same as how-to: one purpose, ~650 words, scannable headings, explicit surfaces.

## Article structure
Title → Issue overview → Article purpose → Before you begin → Step 1 → Step 2... → Related articles

## Writing each section

**Title** — always begins with "Troubleshoot"; plain language; one issue per article
- Example: "Troubleshoot Missing Orders with Brand A KDS"

**Issue overview** — 1-3 sentences, present tense; identify the problem for the seller

**Article purpose** — explain scope; link related concepts; provide reassurance when possible
- Use callout (no heading) for urgent info or blocking actions

**Before you begin** — bullet list, present tense; address:
- When to troubleshoot (after hours preferred; avoid peak hours)
- Multiple device scenarios (troubleshoot each device separately)
- Data preservation (offline mode payments — connect to internet first)
- Platform deprecations or constraints

**Steps** — ordered least invasive to most invasive
- Each step: accordion fragment with "Step #: [verb phrase]" header
- Add introductory sentence setting context for the step
- Ordinal numbers for sub-items; bold UI text; present tense; verb-first
- Dashboard: "Sign in to Brand A Dashboard and go to [path]."
- App: "Open the [POS app] and select…" — SuperPOS modes first (full service, quick service, bar, retail, bookings, services), then legacy apps
- If all modes use same steps: "Point of sale app" header, omit mode names
- Don't repeat surface link within a single section

## Hyperlinks (same as how-to)
Full article title; "Learn how to [article]" or "go to [surface]"; no "click here/here/learn more"; instruction items only.

## Related articles
H2 "Related articles" — bulleted, full title hyperlinked, max 5.
