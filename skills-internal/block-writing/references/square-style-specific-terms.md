# Brand A Style: Specific Terms

## App names
Brand A Point of Sale app, Brand A Dashboard app, Brand A Team app, Hardware Kiosk app, Brand A KDS app

## A–Z Term Reference

**Automated savings** ✅ — not "automatic savings"

**barcode** ✅ — not "bar code"

**cancellation / canceling / canceled** ✅ — not cancelling/cancelled

**Cash flow** — always two words: "cash flow" ✅ not "cashflow"

**deactivate** — always pair with "account": "Your account has been deactivated" ✅ not "you're deactivated"

**Dip slot** — where chip cards are inserted; adjusts regionally outside US

**email** ✅ — not e-mail

**flyer** ✅ — not flier

**gray** ✅ — not grey

**Help Center** (capitalized) ✅ — not help center

**internet** (lowercase) ✅ — not "the Internet"

**Limited-time offer** — do not state expiration date in loan offer comms

**Loan Agreement** — always capitalized (proper noun in legal documents)

**log in / log on**
- "log on" = to visit a website; "log in" = sign in with credentials
- "login" (noun), "log in to" (verb) ✅ — not "Log-in", "Log onto", "Log into"

**meetup** ✅ — not "meet-up"

**OK** ✅ — not ok, okay, O.K.

**peek** ✅ — not peak ("Get a sneak peek")

**pre-authorization** / **pre-qualify** — use hyphen ✅ not "preauthorization"

**Privacy Policy** — capitalized (proper noun) ✅

**push notifications** — omit interaction instructions ("swipe for more", "tap for details")

**reapply** — prefer "apply again"; if used, no hyphen: "reapply" ✅ not "re-apply"

**Rebate / refund** ✅ — not "reimburse"

**repayment rate** ✅ — not "re-payment rate"

**sign-on** (noun/adj) / **sign on** (verb)

**tap** — always use for in-app actions; ❌ "press," "click," "use," or "hit" the button (in-app)

**Terms of Service** — capitalized (proper noun) ✅

**Twitter/X** — "Twitter" capitalized; "tweet" lowercase; still called tweets
