# Afterpay on Cash App: UI Patterns and Research Insights

## UI Implementation

**Progressive disclosure**: Start with core benefits → reveal payment details at appropriate steps → show full terms during confirmation

**Notification standards**:
- Clear benefit statement; specific merchant context; action-oriented language; consistent branding
- Supporting details: payment schedule when relevant; original transaction context; clear next steps

**Activity feed display**:
- Original transaction: merchant name + logo, transaction amount, payment method, date/time
- Afterpay transaction: Status "Changed to Afterpay", original details preserved, clear payment schedule

**UI patterns**:
- CTAs: clear action-oriented language; show specific benefits; follow established hierarchies
- Avoid: "convert" or "transfer" (too technical); hidden payment details; inconsistent iconography

**Visual hierarchy**: Primary = merchant details + payment info; Supporting = original payment method, dates, status indicators

---

## User Research Insights

**Cash App user profile**:
- Avg income $51k | Avg age 34 | Financial literacy score 0.42 | High money stress
- Value simplicity; seek clear communication; need confidence-building; prefer immediate benefits
- Pain points: fear of debt, low financial confidence, uncertainty about terms

**Afterpay awareness**: 12% aware of Cash App-Afterpay connection; 46% familiar with Afterpay; 40% express interest

**What messaging works**:
- Clear, immediate benefits; specific use cases; transparent terms; no credit check messaging
- "with Afterpay" language; clear payment schedules; control messaging
- Progressive information disclosure; simple explanations

**Avoid**: Complex financial terms; unclear money movement; vague benefits; technical jargon
