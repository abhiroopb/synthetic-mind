# Product Voice: Afterpay, Tidal, Bitkey, Proto

## Afterpay
- **Voice**: Modern and approachable; empowering (smart spending); clear about payment structure
- **Audience**: Shoppers (often younger); budget-conscious; online and in-store
- **Terminology**: "installments" (4 equal payments), "pay over time", "interest-free" (when paid on time)

## Tidal
- **Voice**: Artist-focused; music-first; passionate about sound quality and artist equity
- **Audience**: Music fans; audiophiles; people who care about artist compensation
- **Key messaging**: High-quality audio; artist-owned; direct support to artists

## Bitkey
- **Voice**: Security-focused but not paranoid; educational (explaining Bitcoin concepts); empowering
- **Audience**: Bitcoin users (beginners to advanced); security-conscious; self-custody advocates
- **Terminology**: "Bitcoin" (not "crypto" or "BTC"), "recovery" (accessing funds if device lost), "self-custody" (you control your keys)

## Proto
- **Voice**: Forward-thinking; experimental; transparent about exploratory nature
- **Audience**: Early adopters; people interested in emerging Block products
- **Key messaging**: Experimental products; shaping the future of financial tools; your feedback matters

---

## Cross-Product Guidelines

### Maintain Core Block Voice
All products should feel like they're part of the Block family:
- Crisp (concise and useful)
- Self-assured (confident, not arrogant)
- Fluent (adapt to context and audience)
- Upfront (transparent, especially about problems)

### Adapt for Audience
- **Consumer products** (Cash App, Afterpay, Tidal): More casual, conversational, approachable
- **Business products** (Square): Professional but helpful, business-appropriate
- **Technical products** (Bitkey): Educational, security-focused, empowering
- **Experimental products** (Proto): Forward-thinking, transparent about experimental nature

### When in Doubt
1. Start with core Block voice principles
2. Adapt formality level for audience (consumer vs. business)
3. Use clear, simple language
4. Be transparent and helpful
