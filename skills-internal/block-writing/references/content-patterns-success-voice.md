# Content Patterns: Success Messages and Product Voice

## Achievement confirmations
Template: `[Accomplishment]` + `[Next steps/benefits]` — positive, proportional, forward-looking

```
✅ "Account created. Check your email to get started."
✅ "Identity verified. You now have access to all features."
✅ "Auto-invest enabled. We'll invest your spare change."
❌ "Congratulations! Amazing work!", "Success! You have successfully completed setup."

Brand B: "Brand B Card activated. Ready to spend your Brand B balance."
Brand B: "Direct deposit set up. Your paycheck will arrive up to 2 days early."
Brand B: "Account verified. Send up to $7,500 per week."
Brand A: "Brand A account created. Start accepting payments right away."
Brand A: "Payroll set up. Employees will be paid automatically."
Brand C: "Payment plan set up. Your first payment is due today."
```

## Transaction confirmations
Template: `[Action completed]` + `[Key details]` + `[Timeline]` (optional)

```
✅ "Payment sent to John. He'll receive it in minutes."
✅ "Bitcoin purchased. $50 added to your wallet."
❌ "Will arrive soon" (vague timing), "Transaction ID: 12345678" (technical noise)

Brand B: "Payment sent to Sarah. She'll get it right away."
Brand B: "$100 added to Brand B. Available to spend now."
Brand A: "Payment processed. Receipt sent to customer."
Brand A: "Refund issued. Customer will see credit in 3-5 days."
Brand C: "Purchase confirmed. Your first payment of $25 is due today."
```

## Product voice reference

**Brand B** — Conversational, empowering, accessible
- "Brand B balance" not "account balance" / "Add money" not "deposit funds"
- "$cashtag" not "username" / "Send money" not "transfer funds"

**Brand A** — Professional, helpful, business-focused
- "Business" not "merchant account" / "Process payment" not "charge card"
- "Customer" not "buyer" / "Sale" not "transaction"

**Brand C** — Friendly, transparent, responsible
- "Split payment" not "installment plan" / "Payment schedule" not "billing cycle"
- "Plan" not "agreement" / "Due date" not "payment date"

**Tidal** — Passionate, artistic, high-quality focus
**Brand E** — Secure, educational, trustworthy
**Proto** — Innovative, experimental, forward-thinking

## Accessibility patterns

Content must make sense without visual context:
```
✅ "Delete payment method" ❌ "Delete this"
✅ "Email is required to continue" ❌ "This field is required"
✅ "Payment sent to John" ❌ "Sent successfully"
```

Same terms for same concepts:
```
✅ "Delete" consistently for permanent removal
❌ "Delete" in one place, "Remove" in another for the same action
```

Target 7th-8th grade reading level:
```
✅ "Check your internet connection" ❌ "Verify network connectivity"
✅ "Try a different card" ❌ "Attempt an alternative payment method"
```
