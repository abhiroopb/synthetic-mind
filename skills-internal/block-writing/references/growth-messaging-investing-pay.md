# Cash App Growth Messaging: Investing, Pay, and Security

## Stocks
Buy stocks for as little as $1; no commission fees; fractional shares; individual stocks only (no index funds/mutual funds).

- Use "stocks" — not "shares" or "investments" (keeps it simple for smaller investors)
- Don't capitalize "stocks" or "invest"
- Use "buy stocks" or "invest" as verbs
- Don't promise growth: "Grow your money" needs a hedge — we can't guarantee results
- No "build your portfolio" (most customers are smaller investors, not power traders)
- No commission fees, but rare other fees can apply — don't say "no fees"

✅ "Buy stocks with as little as $1" / "Buy stocks with no commission fees"
❌ "Buy stocks with no fees" / "Grow your money by investing in stocks" / "Build your portfolio"

**Disclosure**: Brokerage services by Cash App Investing LLC, member FINRA/SIPC. Investing involves risk; you may lose money. [Full fractional shares disclaimer when applicable]

## Bitcoin
Buy bitcoin for as little as $1; also sell, receive, and send from other wallets; "get paid in bitcoin" with direct deposit.

- Always "**bitcoin**" (or Bitcoin at sentence start) — never "crypto" or "cryptocurrency"
- Bitcoin is both singular and plural
- Use "**buy**" — not "invest"; "invest" raises concerns about price volatility
- Don't use "grow" as it relates to bitcoin (compliance prefers against implying guaranteed outcomes)
- Don't say "Cash App is the easiest way" (avoid absolutes and superlatives)
- We don't typically get into sats or "part of a bitcoin"

✅ "Buy bitcoin with as little as $1" / "When you buy bitcoin with Cash App, you actually own it"
❌ "Invest in bitcoin and watch it grow" / "Buy crypto" / "Buy bitcoin and stocks" via Round Ups (one destination)

**Disclosure**: "Bitcoin services are offered by Block Inc. Purchasing bitcoin involves risk; you may lose money."

## Cash App Pay
Pay from Cash balance at checkout in-person (QR code) or online; no card number needed; available at select merchants.

- **Cash App Pay** is a branded term — every word capitalized
- To avoid repetition, don't use "pay" as the main verb: use "choose", "use", or "select"
- Not available everywhere — don't imply universal acceptance
- Awareness is still growing — don't assume customers know it exists

✅ "When it's time to pay, choose Cash App Pay"
✅ "Cash App Pay works at places you know and love"
❌ "Pay with Cash App Pay" (too much pay) / "Cash App Pay works everywhere" (not available everywhere)
❌ "Choose Cash App Pay to pay for your Lyft ride with your Cash balance" (too much cash)

## Security
Build confidence by weaving in relevant security features. Cash App uses proactive features to prevent fraud before it happens.

**Platform features** (always on):
- 24/7 fraud detection and proactive security
- Real-time notifications for suspicious activity
- Identity verification

**Customer-controlled features**:
- **Card lock**: lock card instantly if lost or stolen
- **Contact syncing**: helps protect against fraud; control incoming requests
- **Security lock**: require face, fingerprint, or PIN to move money or unlock app

**Key rules**:
- Never say "advanced security features" (compliance guidance—say "security features" or "proactive security features")
- Don't say "your money is safe with us" or imply guaranteed protection
- Use "secure" and "safe" interchangeably—both are fine
- Don't mention FDIC insurance in campaigns (temporary guidance due to bank partners)
- Don't reference "Face ID" or "Touch ID" unless campaign is specifically for iOS customers

✅ "Cash App uses proactive security features to prevent fraud before it can happen"
✅ "With security lock, you can require your face, fingerprint, or PIN to move money"
❌ "Cash App uses advanced security features" / "With security lock, require Face ID or Touch ID" (iOS-specific)
