# Brand C on Brand B: Terminology and Compliance

## Core Brand Terms

| Term | Use | Don't Use |
|------|-----|-----------|
| **Brand C** | As a branded feature within Brand B | As a verb; "Brand B Brand C" |
| **with Brand C** | For feature and flow messaging | Possessive: "Brand B's Brand C" |
| **pay over time** | As descriptor to explain Brand C | — |
| **Activity** | For Brand C transactions in process or completed | — |

## Transaction and Payment Terms

| Term | Use | Don't Use |
|------|-----|-----------|
| **Switched to Brand C** | For conversion confirmations: "$61.40 Switched to Brand C" | "Changed to", "Moved to" |
| **Purchase** | To refer to bought items/services | "Order" |
| **Recent purchases** | "Switch your recent purchases to Brand C" | "Past purchases" |
| **Payment** | To refer to scheduled payments | "Repayment", "installment" |
| **Pay / Pay now** | For payment CTAs | "Repay", "repay early", "pay an installment" |
| **Total owed** | "$150.30 total owed" | "Total amount due", "total due", "total owing" |
| **Available to spend** | Max amount available at merchants | — |
| **Available to get back** | Max total for Retro transactions | — |
| **Spending limit** | Internal concepts only | In customer-facing communications |

## Compliance: Trigger Terms

**Payment structure terms require disclosure**:
- Only use "Pay in 4" or "Pay in 6" on screens dedicated to education with proper disclosure text
- Use "Pay in installments" for general marketing messages
- Can use specific payment numbers in product flows when showing actual loan terms (in context of active loan terms review)

✅ "Pay in installments with Brand C" (general marketing)
✅ Specific payment numbers when displaying actual loan schedule

❌ Specific numbers without proper disclosure in educational/marketing content

**Money movement language**:
✅ "Get money back to your Cash balance when you switch to Brand C"
❌ "Get money back when you switch to Brand C" (unclear destination)

Always specify the destination for any money movement. When in doubt, consult legal.
