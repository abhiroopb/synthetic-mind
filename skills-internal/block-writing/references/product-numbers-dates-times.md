# Product Numbers: Dates, Times, and Formatting

## Date Formatting
Spell out when possible; comma after day of week; comma after day when year is included:
- `[Day of week], [Month] [Day], [Year]`
- ✅ "Sunday, February 23, 2025" | "February 2018"
- ❌ "Sun February 23 2025" | "February, 2026"

Abbreviations (when space is limited, no periods): Jan–Dec | Mon–Sun

Very limited space: MM/DD/YY format (check with localization for non-US):
- ✅ "04/21/18" | ❌ "04-21-25"

No ordinal numbers in dates:
- ✅ "This invoice will be sent on Nov 21."
- ❌ "This invoice will be sent on Nov 21st."

Date ranges: en dash without spaces; state shared details once:
- ✅ "Sep 4–Dec 11, 2025" | "September 4–5, 2025"
- ❌ "Sep 4, 2023 - Dec 11, 2023" | "September 4 - September 5"

## Telephone Numbers
Hyphens; no parentheses around area code; no preceding "1":
- ✅ "415-555-5555" | "800-726-CASH (2274)"
- ❌ "1 (415) 555-5555" | "800-726-CASH" (no numeric equivalent)
- Use "extension" or "ext." (not "x") | Use "toll-free number" (not "1-800 number")

## Time of Day
Numerals + space + AM/PM (no periods, no spaces between letters):
- In sentence: can omit :00 → "The store closes at 6 PM."
- Elsewhere include :00 → "11:30 AM"
- ✅ "11:30 AM", "The store opens at 11:30 AM."
- ❌ "11:30 a.m.", "6pm", "11:30AM"

## Time Zones
Abbreviate as PT, MT, CT, ET (no periods; no daylight savings refs):
- ✅ "7 AM PT" | "Current time zone: Pacific time" | "11:30 AM PT"
- ❌ "7 AM PST" | "Current time zone: Pacific Time" | "11:30 AM Pacific"

## Time Duration
Space allows: spell out → "This appointment will last 2 hours and 30 minutes."
Limited space: "7 hr 45 min worked" (not "7 hrs 45 mins")
Very limited: "8h 12m" (not "8h12")
Omit 0-value hours or minutes when unneeded.

## Timestamps and Relative Dates
| Elapsed | Format |
|---------|--------|
| Today | "Today" |
| Yesterday | "Yesterday" |
| 2–6 days ago | Day name: "Monday", "Tuesday" |
| 7 days–10 months ago | "June 20", "May 11" |
| Over 10 months ago | "Aug 2022", "Jan 2021" |
