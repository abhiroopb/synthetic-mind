# Brand B Growth Writing: Email Guidelines

## Overview
Brand B growth campaigns (emails, house ads, push notifications, landing pages) follow UX writing principles—same terminology, voice, and customer empowerment. Campaign success is tied to performance, so understand KPIs and goals before writing. Guidelines help, not limit—use your UX instincts.

---

## Email: Subject
- First ~40 characters visible on mobile
- 8 words or fewer (exceptions: numbers, short words)
- No periods at end of one-sentence subject lines
- Front load action or value; avoid starting with "Brand B" (it's in the From field)
- Avoid rhetorical/hypothetical questions that open the door to say "no"

✅ "Earn up to 4.5% interest on savings" | "File your taxes for literally $0"
❌ "A tax refund could be in your future" (opt-out) | "Do even more with Brand B" (vague)

## Email: Preheader
- First ~40 characters visible on mobile; 8 words or fewer
- No periods at end; don't repeat or paraphrase the subject
- Use as extension of subject or compelling preview of what's inside
- Front load action or value

✅ "Make your account officially yours" | "It's free and a better way to file"
❌ "By verifying your identity, your account can be officially yours" (important part hidden)

## Email: Headline
- 45 characters or less (avoids 4 lines on mobile)
- Don't repeat the subject (it stacks on top)
- Clear, actionable, memorable — if someone only saw subject + headline + first sentence, would it cover basics?

✅ "Save for what you want faster" | "Your banking just got better"
❌ "Saving is better with a Brand B Card" (company value, not customer) | starts with "Brand B"

## Email: Starter Template

**Subject**: Clear, actionable in 8 words or less (~40 chars)

**Preheader**: Extension of subject, doesn't repeat it (~40 chars)

**Headline**: Clear, actionable, doesn't repeat subject (~45 chars)

**Body — Intro/Body**:
- ≤3 sentences per paragraph; ≤3 paragraphs before bullets/cards/CTA
- Efficient, memorable; show personality when it makes sense
- Finish with a sentence, not a phrase + colon leading into bullets

**Body — Bullets/Cards**:
- Topic: 5 words or fewer (no periods)
- Description: 200 characters or fewer (normal punctuation)
- Parallel structure preferred but don't force it

**CTA**:
- 3 words or fewer
- Start with a verb; follow standard UX CTA rules
