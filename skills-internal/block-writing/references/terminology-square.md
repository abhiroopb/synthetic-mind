# Square Terminology

Always use exact capitalization and spelling. Check "Avoid" column for deprecated terms.

## Product names

| Use | Avoid | Notes |
|-----|-------|-------|
| **Square** | "the Square", "square", "SQ" | No article unless part of a specific product name |
| **Square Point of Sale** | "Square POS", "SPOS", "Point-of-Sale" | Spell out in customer-facing content |
| **Square for Retail** | "Retail", "Square Retail" | — |
| **Square Online** | "Square online", "Online Store" | — |
| **Square Payroll** | "Payroll", "Square payroll" | — |
| **Square Terminal** | "Terminal", "the terminal" | Hardware product name |
| **Square Reader** | "Reader", "card reader" (for the specific product) | Capitalize when referring to the Square hardware |

## Audience terms

| Use | Avoid | Notes |
|-----|-------|-------|
| **sellers** | "users" (too generic in business context) | Preferred over "merchants" in most contexts |
| **merchants** | — | Acceptable; "sellers" is preferred |
| **business owners** | "entrepreneurs" (unless specifically appropriate) | — |
| **customers** | "buyers", "shoppers" (unless context requires) | Refers to the seller's customers, not Square's |

## Business operations terms

| Use | Avoid | Notes |
|-----|-------|-------|
| **items** / products | "SKUs" (most customer-facing contexts) | "SKUs" only in technical/advanced contexts |
| **staff** / team members | "employees" (unless about employment status) | More inclusive terminology |
| **locations** | "stores" (unless specifically retail) | Generic term works for all business types |
