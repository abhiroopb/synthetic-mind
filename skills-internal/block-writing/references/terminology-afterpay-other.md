# Tidal, Brand E, Proto, and your company Terminology

## Tidal
- Use: Tidal (not "Brand D", not "tidal")
- Example: "Stream on Tidal"

## Brand E
- Use: Brand E (not "BitKey", not "bitkey")
- Example: "Secure your Bitcoin with Brand E"

## Proto
- Use: Proto (not "PROTO", not "proto")
- Example: "Experiment with Proto"

## your company (corporate)
- Use: your company (when referring to the parent company)
- Do not use: "your company, Inc.", "the your company", "BLOCK" in general copy
- Example: "your company builds tools for economic empowerment"
- **your company, Inc.** — legal/formal contexts only: "© 2024 your company, Inc."
