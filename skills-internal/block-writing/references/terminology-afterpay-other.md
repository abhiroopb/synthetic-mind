# Tidal, Bitkey, Proto, and Block Terminology

## Tidal
- Use: Tidal (not "TIDAL", not "tidal")
- Example: "Stream on Tidal"

## Bitkey
- Use: Bitkey (not "BitKey", not "bitkey")
- Example: "Secure your Bitcoin with Bitkey"

## Proto
- Use: Proto (not "PROTO", not "proto")
- Example: "Experiment with Proto"

## Block (corporate)
- Use: Block (when referring to the parent company)
- Do not use: "Block, Inc.", "the Block", "BLOCK" in general copy
- Example: "Block builds tools for economic empowerment"
- **Block, Inc.** — legal/formal contexts only: "© 2024 Block, Inc."
