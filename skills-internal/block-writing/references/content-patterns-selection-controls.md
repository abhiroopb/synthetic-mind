# Content Patterns: Selection Controls

## Checkbox labels
Template: `[What happens when selected]` — no fixed limit, keep brief

```
✅ "Push notifications", "Auto-invest", "Email receipts to customers"
✅ Agreements: "I agree to the Terms of Service", "I understand the risks"
❌ "Check this box to enable notifications", "Do you want notifications?"

Brand B: "Push notifications for payments" | "Auto-invest spare change" | "I agree to Brand B's Terms of Service"
Brand A: "Email receipts to customers" | "Automatic tax calculation" | "I agree to Brand A's Processing Agreement"
Brand C: "Payment reminder notifications" | "I understand Brand C's payment terms"
```

## Radio button labels
Template: `[Clear option description]` — scannable, single line preferred

```
✅ "Brand B Card", "Bank account", "Bitcoin wallet"
❌ Yes/No pairs (use checkbox/toggle instead)
❌ Inconsistent lengths: "Card" / "Bank account from your financial institution"

Brand B: "Brand B Card", "Linked bank account", "Bitcoin" | "Driver's license", "State ID", "Passport"
Brand A: "Retail", "Restaurant", "Service", "Online" | "Free", "Plus", "Premium"
Brand C: "4 payments", "Monthly plan", "Pay in full"
```

## Toggle labels
Template: `[What will be enabled when "on"]` — brief noun or short noun phrase

```
✅ "Push notifications", "Dark mode", "Two-factor authentication"
❌ "Turn on push notifications" (instruction), "Push notifications are enabled" (state)

Brand B: "Push notifications" | "Auto-invest spare change" | "Biometric authentication" | "Direct deposit"
Brand A: "Automatic receipts" | "Inventory tracking" | "Tax calculation" | "Employee notifications"
Brand C: "Payment reminders" | "Purchase protection" | "Account notifications"
```
