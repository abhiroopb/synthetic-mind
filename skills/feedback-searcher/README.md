# Feedback searcher

> Search and synthesize seller feedback across support transcripts, sales calls, Slack channels, product demand tracking, and internal docs.

## What it does

This skill searches across multiple feedback channels in parallel — customer service phone and chat transcripts, account manager call recordings, Slack feedback channels, a structured product demand tracker, and internal documentation. It launches parallel subagents for each data source, then synthesizes the results into a comprehensive report with themes, sentiment, representative quotes, and recommendations.

## Usage

Use when researching seller feedback on any topic. The skill asks clarifying questions (topic, time period, seller segment, insight type) before launching searches across all sources simultaneously.

## Examples

- "What are sellers saying about invoicing in the last 90 days?"
- "Search for churn signals related to the checkout experience"
- "Find product requests related to cash rounding from upmarket sellers"

## Why it was created

Seller feedback is fragmented across support tools, sales recordings, Slack, and product intake forms. This skill unifies the search across all channels and produces an actionable synthesis, turning hours of manual research into minutes.
