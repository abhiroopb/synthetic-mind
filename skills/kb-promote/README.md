# KB Promote

> Graduate high-value knowledge notes into permanent rules or new skills.

## What it does

KB Promote is the final layer of the knowledge flywheel. It identifies distilled notes that have proven stable over time — referenced across multiple sessions, never contradicted, and derived from strong evidence — and proposes promoting them into permanent configuration rules or new skills. Each candidate is scored on stability signals and classified into the right destination: a behavioral rule, an enhancement to an existing skill, or a brand new skill.

## Usage

Run this skill after a distillation pass or whenever you want to formalize learned behaviors. All promotions require explicit user approval — nothing is auto-promoted.

**Trigger phrases:**
- "Promote"
- "Graduate my notes"
- "What should be permanent?"
- "Review promotion candidates"
- "Promote the merchant lookup workflow"

## Examples

- `"Promote"` — Reviews all distilled notes, scores them on stability, and presents ranked candidates with approve/skip/edit options.
- `"What's ready to promote?"` — Shows candidates and their scores without taking action.
- `"Force promote everything"` — Promotes all candidates scoring above the threshold without individual approval (still shows a summary report).

## Why it was created

Knowledge captured during sessions is only useful if it persists. KB Promote closes the loop by turning battle-tested observations into permanent behavioral rules, ensuring the agent gets smarter over time without manual configuration.
