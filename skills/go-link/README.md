# Go link

> Translate internal go/ shortlinks to their full URLs and fetch their content.

## What it does

This skill resolves internal go/ shortlinks (like `go/benefits` or `go/oncall`) to their full URLs. It can either navigate to the page interactively or fetch the content directly, depending on what you need. It also reports the translated URL so you always know what was accessed.

## Usage

Use when you encounter or mention go/ links, internal shortlinks, or need to navigate to or fetch content from go/ URLs. The skill handles the translation automatically.

## Examples

- "Open go/benefits"
- "What's at go/oncall/schedule?"
- "Fetch the content of go/wiki/engineering"

## Why it was created

Internal go/ links require a browser extension to resolve, and agents don't have that extension installed. This skill bridges that gap by translating shortlinks to full URLs automatically.
