================================================================================

TABLE NAME: APP_PA.APP_SQS.EMPLOYEES
DATABASE: APP_PA
SCHEMA: APP_PA.APP_SQS
VERIFICATION STATUS: VERIFIED (Human reviewed and approved)
BRAND: Square
TABLE TYPE: Analytics
TOTAL RECENT USERS: 64 active users (past 91 days)

PRIMARY KEY: EMPLOYEE_TOKEN

TABLE PURPOSE:
This table contains comprehensive employee data for Square Merchants. It tracks 
employee creation, setup, permissions, activities, and first/last engagement 
with various Square and Staff features. The table is designed for analyzing 
employee onboarding, engagement, and utilization of Square's team management, 
payroll, appointments, shifts, and other business management features.

KEY BUSINESS CONCEPTS
================================================================================

ACTIVE EMPLOYEE DEFINITIONS:
The table supports 4 tiers of "active" status:

1. ENABLED: A non-account owner employee has is_enabled = TRUE. This is a production label and is only as of current date. DONT use for historical tracking. 
2. ENGAGED OR UTILIZED: last_engaged_ts OR last_utilized_ts within past 30 days
3. ENGAGED: last_engaged_ts within past 30 days
4. UTILIZED: last_utilized_ts within past 30 days

For historical metrics, use deactivated_ts to determine if employee was 
"enabled" during a specific timeframe.

EMPLOYEE ONBOARDING MILESTONES:
The table tracks key setup events anchored on employee_created_ts and windowed 
to FIRST_* feature engagement timestamps (typically 7 or 30 days):

SQL: COUNT(DISTINCT CASE WHEN FIRST_*_TS BETWEEN employee_created_ts AND DATEADD('days', 7, employee_created_ts) THEN employee_token END) AS onboarding_rate 

- Employee creation (employee_created_ts)
- Job assignment (first_job_assigned_ts)
- Invitation sent/accepted (first_invitation_sent_ts, first_invitation_accepted_ts)
- First login (first_login_ts)
- Payroll setup (payroll_employee_created_ts, payroll_invitation_sent_ts)
- Payout method setup (first_payout_method_setup_ts)
- Feature engagement (shifts, appointments, team app, etc.)

EMPLOYEE CREATION FLOWS (employee_created_in):

COMMON QUERY PATTERNS
================================================================================

COUNTING CURRENT ENGAGED OR UTILIZED EMPLOYEES (Most Common Pattern):
```sql
SELECT 
    COUNT(DISTINCT employee_token) AS total_employees,
    COUNT(DISTINCT merchant_token) AS total_merchants
FROM app_pa.app_sqs.employees
WHERE is_enabled = TRUE # not deleted from system 
  AND (is_owner_role = FALSE OR is_owner_role IS NULL)
  AND employee_created_in IN (
      'api', 'appointments_legacy', 'appointments_unified_onboarding',
      'auth_rep_dashboard', 'auth_rep_register_android', 'auth_rep_register_ios',
      'auth_rep_register_ipados', 'dashboard_legacy', 'payroll_legacy',
      'team_applet_register-android', 'team_applet_register-ios',
      'team_applet_restaurant-pos-android', 'team_applet_restaurant-pos-ios',
      'team_applet_retail-pos-android', 'team_applet_retail-pos-ios',
      'unified_onboarding'
  )
  AND (
      TO_DATE(last_engaged_ts) >= DATEADD('day', -31, CURRENT_TIMESTAMP())
      OR TO_DATE(last_utilized_ts) >= DATEADD('day', -31, CURRENT_TIMESTAMP())
  );
```

FILTERING NON-OWNER EMPLOYEES:
Always use: (is_owner_role = FALSE OR is_owner_role IS NULL)

PAYROLL EMPLOYEES:
Filter by: employee_facet_id IS NOT NULL

EMPLOYEE ONBOARDING ANALYSIS:

SELECT

BUSINESS CONTEXT: EMPLOYEE ONBOARDING
================================================================================

EMPLOYEE ONBOARDING JOURNEY:
Square Staff focuses on seamless employee onboarding across multiple touchpoints:

1. EMPLOYEE CREATION
   - Sellers create employees via Dashboard, POS, Team App, or Payroll
   - Unified onboarding flow consolidates creation across products
   - Appointments merged their flow with Staff's Team list (Nov 2024)
   - 85% of appointment employees made bookable (up from 68%)

2. INVITATION & SETUP
   - Invitations sent via email or phone (first_invitation_sent_method)
   - Employees accept and create accounts
   - Job titles and wage information assigned
   - Permissions and access rights configured

3. PAYROLL ONBOARDING
   - Separate payroll invitation flow (payroll_invitation_sent_ts)
   - EIN setup required for payroll activation
   - Employee profile and tax page visits tracked
   - Payout method setup (Cash App, direct deposit, manual check)
   - Critical metric: Time from invitation to first payout

4. FEATURE ENGAGEMENT
   - First login to various platforms (mobile POS, dashboard, team app)
   - First use of shifts, appointments, communications
   - First attributable sale or transaction
   - Ongoing engagement tracked via last_engaged_ts and last_utilized_ts

STAFF SETUP GUIDE:
- Launched to improve onboarding experience
- Guides sellers through employee creation, payroll setup, shifts configuration
- A/B testing shows impact on setup rates and feature adoption
- V2 prioritizes Team Member creation before Payroll setup

KEY ONBOARDING METRICS:
- Employee creation rate (7D, 30D)
- EIN setup rate (for payroll)
- First payout timing
- Feature adoption rates (shifts, appointments, team app)
- Cash App linking and payout adoption

STRATEGIC INITIATIVES (2025-2026)
================================================================================

EMPLOYEE NETWORK STRATEGY:
Square is building an "employee network" to connect buyers, staff, and sellers:

1. CASH APP INTEGRATION
   - Move employee experience into Cash App
   - Team Member Accounts become Cash App accounts
   - Team App experience as Cash App applet
   - Goal: $8B+ addressable annual inflows from Square to Cash App

2. DAILY TIP PAYOUTS
   - GA launch of instant tip payouts to Cash App
   - Automated daily payouts without seller intervention
   - Free tip payouts for employees
   - Prerequisite: Next-gen tip pooling and attribution

3. TIP MANAGEMENT
   - Multiple tip pools support
   - Points-based pooling
   - Attribution improvements (46% of tips currently unattributed)
   - Day parts, cash tips, auto gratuity, tip-outs

4. PAYROLL SCALING
   - KYB integration with PayON
   - Multi-EIN support for sellers with multiple businesses
   - Churn return capabilities
   - Target: Increase SMB+ F&B Payroll attach rate

5. AI/MANAGERBOT SUPPORT
   - Data and API infrastructure for AI-powered staff management
   - Intelligent interface for managers
   - Automated scheduling, insights, task management

CURRENT CHALLENGES:
- 46% of tip dollars go unattributed to employees
- Only 63% of employees marked as tip eligible (74% in F&B)
- Employee creation doesn't always lead to payroll enrollment
- Mobile POS permission issues (51% encounter "permission required" screen)
- Setup guide effectiveness varies by seller type
