# KB Style Matrix

> Build a communication voice profile by analyzing your Slack messages.

## What it does

KB Style Matrix analyzes ~90 days of your Slack messages across different audience types (team, cross-functional, leadership) and extracts your natural communication patterns. It produces a structured voice profile covering formality, directness, energy, emoji usage, message structure, signature phrases, and audience-specific adaptations. This profile is then used by other skills to match your voice when drafting emails, Slack messages, and documents.

## Usage

Run this skill once during initial setup, then refresh periodically (monthly recommended) to keep the profile current.

**Trigger phrases:**
- "Build my style matrix"
- "Analyze my communication style"
- "Create my voice profile"
- "Refresh my style matrix"

## Examples

- `"Build my style matrix"` — Pulls messages from your most active Slack channels, analyzes patterns across audience types, and saves a structured voice profile.
- `"Refresh my style matrix"` — Rebuilds the profile with fresh data and highlights any shifts (e.g., "Your messages have gotten more direct over the past quarter").

## Why it was created

AI-drafted messages often sound generic. By profiling your actual communication style — including subtle audience adaptations — this skill enables other tools to produce drafts that sound like you, not a robot.
