# Deprecated Terms

Do not use these outdated or incorrect terms:

## Cash App
- ❌ "cash out" → Use "withdraw"
- ❌ "transfer funds" → Use "send" or "withdraw"
- ❌ "Cash Card" (alone) → Use "Cash App Card"
- ❌ "the Cash App" → Use "Cash App"
- ❌ "CashApp" (no space) → Use "Cash App"
- ❌ "cash tag" (two words) → Use "$cashtag"

## Square
- ❌ "users" → Use "sellers", "merchants", or "business owners"
- ❌ "employees" (general use) → Use "staff" or "team members"
- ❌ "SKUs" (customer-facing) → Use "items" or "products"

## General
- ❌ "wallet" → Use product-specific terms (Cash App balance, etc.)
- ❌ "BTC" (in prose) → Use "Bitcoin"

---

## Adding New Terms
1. Place under the appropriate product section in terminology-cash-app.md, terminology-square.md, or terminology-afterpay-other.md
2. Format: Term name, Use/Do not use, Example, Notes
3. Add deprecated replacements here when retiring old terminology
