# Cash App Web Help Center: Article Templates

## Platform notes
- **Web**: logged-out, general audience; **In-app**: mobile browser — consider screen size
- Naming may differ (e.g., "How to Reset your PIN" web vs. "Reset PIN In-App" in-app)
- Break articles at 4,500 chars
- **Media**: gifs for how-to steps; video for long educational content
- **SEO**: sentence case in body/sections; title case in article titles
  - Summary, Meta Title, Meta Description: keep consistent, 82 chars max each

## How-to articles
Goal: step-by-step solution to an issue.

- **Title**: "How to [task]" — 50 chars ideal, 100 max
- **Intro**: 250 chars max
- **Body**: intro → numbered steps → [gif] → Outcome (what's possible once done)
- **Learn More**: related links, max 3
- Meta Description: 82 chars max

## Informational — short form (1,500 chars max)
Goal: educational content on a specific topic.

- **Title**: "What is" / "How do I" / "How to" — 50 chars ideal, 100 max
- **Body**: brief description → 2-3 paragraphs
- **Learn More**: max 3

## Informational — long form (4,500 chars max)
Goal: comprehensive educational content.

- **Title**: 50 chars ideal, 100 max
- **Body**: brief intro (250 chars) → Jump to (max 5 links) → subtitle/body sections
- **Learn More**: max 3

## Issue identification
Goal: explain the "why" behind an issue, not steps to a solution.

- **Title**: "Why is" / "What is" / "How do I" — 50 chars ideal, 100 max
- **Body**: brief intro (250 chars) → subtitle/body sections
- **Related articles**: linked
