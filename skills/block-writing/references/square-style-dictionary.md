# Square Style: Common Words and Dictionary

When writing for or about Square products, use common words. Keep it simple, clear, friendly. Avoid technical terms, slang, or region-specific vocabulary.

## Key Term Guidance

| Term | Use | Avoid |
|------|-----|-------|
| **Square** | noun only; "Square's" as possessive | as a verb |
| **sellers** | primary audience; use "you" when speaking directly | entrepreneurs, clients, merchants (for Square businesses) |
| **customers** | people who buy from sellers | buyers, guests (except F&B context) |
| **guest** | F&B (restaurants) only—interchangeable with diner | in retail or general contexts |
| **users** | only when discussing context of the app | to describe customers, sellers, drivers in general |
| **team members** | seller's personnel | employees (preferred term) |
| **Actions** | stop, cancel, end, exit | abort, terminate |
| **App buttons** | tap (in-app only) | press, click, hit (in-app) |
| **Web buttons** | press, click, or common verbs | made-up action words |
| **app** | specify which app: "Square Point of Sale app", "Square Dashboard app" | just "app" without context |

## Words to Avoid

**Jargon**: leverage, utilize, synergies, paradigm, turnkey, best-in-class, cutting-edge, world-class

**Overpromises**: guaranteed, always, never, perfect, flawless, your money is safe with us, no risk, foolproof

**Financial hype**: get rich, easy money

**Casual slang**: cool, awesome, rad, killer app, hustle, disrupt, game-changing

**Internal/technical**: LFC, API callouts, flow states, internal shorthand, "ecosystem of apps"

**Vague**: thing, stuff, feature-rich, robust
