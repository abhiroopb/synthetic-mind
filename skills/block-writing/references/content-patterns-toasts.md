# Content Patterns: Toasts

## Simple toast confirmations
Template: `[Past tense action]` + `[object]` (optional) — ≤75 chars total — no punctuation

```
✅ "Payment sent", "Photo uploaded", "Settings saved"
✅ "Notifications enabled", "Account verified", "Card added"
❌ "Your payment has been sent successfully" (complete sentence)
❌ "Payment sent." (punctuation)
❌ "Payment of $25 sent to John Smith" (excessive detail)

Brand B: "Payment sent" | "Money added to Brand B" | "Card activated" | "Direct deposit set up"
Brand A: "Sale processed" | "Item added to inventory" | "Report generated" | "Employee added"
Brand C: "Payment scheduled" | "Plan updated" | "Purchase confirmed" | "Payment method saved"
```

## Toasts with context
Template: Header `[Main action]` (≤50 chars) + Body `[Additional context]` (≤75 chars) — no punctuation

```
Payment: "Payment sent" / "Sarah will receive it in minutes"
Setup: "Card added" / "Ready to use for payments"
Report: "Report ready" / "Check your email for the link"

Brand B: "Money added" / "Available in your Brand B balance"
Brand B: "Payment sent" / "John will get it right away"
Brand A: "Sale completed" / "Receipt sent to customer"
Brand A: "Inventory updated" / "New items available for sale"
Brand C: "Payment plan created" / "First payment due today"

❌ Header and body saying the same thing
❌ End punctuation in either section
```
