# Product Mechanics: Text and Formatting

## Abbreviations and Acronyms
Spell out full term on first use followed by abbreviation in parentheses — unless commonly known (FAQ, IRS, ATM, URL):
- ✅ "What's your Employer Identification Number (EIN)?" | "Find an ATM"
- ❌ "What's your EIN?" | "Find an automated teller machine (ATM)"

Plurals: lowercase "s" without apostrophe:
- ✅ "ATMs", "APIs" | ❌ "ATM's", "API's"

Date abbreviations (no periods): Jan, Feb, Mar, Apr, May, Jun, Jul, Aug, Sep, Oct, Nov, Dec | Mon, Tue, Wed, Thu, Fri, Sat, Sun

Time abbreviations (lowercase, no period, no space after numeral): day, wk, mo, yr, hr, min, sec → abbreviated as d, wk, mo, yr, hr, min, sec; extremely limited space: h, m, s
- ✅ "Expires in 10 hours 5 minutes", "7 hr 45 min worked"
- ❌ "Expires in 10 hours, 5 minutes", "7 hrs 45 mins worked"

## Active Voice
Use active voice whenever possible:
- ✅ "We'll send you a code" | ❌ "A code will be sent to you"
- Passive OK for confirmation messages: "Your payment was sent"

## Capitalization
Sentence case for all headings, buttons, navigation, form fields:
- ✅ "Add a bank account", "Cash App settings"
- ❌ "Add A Bank Account", "Cash App Settings"

## Contractions
Use common contractions for conversational tone:
- ✅ "We can't process this payment", "You're all set", "It's on the way"
- ❌ "We cannot process this payment", "You are all set"

Avoid awkward contractions:
- ✅ "It will be ready soon", "They would like to connect"
- ❌ "It'll be ready soon", "They'd like to connect"

## Bolded Text
Only bold things people select (UI elements); don't bold for emphasis:
- ✅ Tap **Send**, Go to **Settings**
- ❌ "This is **really important**"

## Emojis
Sparingly; place at end of sentence without additional punctuation; consider localization:
- ✅ "Your money is on the way 🎉"
- ❌ "Your money is on the way 🎉." | Avoid mid-sentence emojis

## Plurals
Don't use (s) to show plurals; pick plural form or rewrite:
- ✅ "Add your bank accounts" or "Add a bank account or multiple accounts"
- ❌ "Add your bank account(s)"

## Prepositions
Use correct prepositions for interface references:
- "In" for sections: "In the Settings section"
- "On" for pages: "On the Payment page"
- "With" for apps: "With Cash App"

## Trademarks
Don't include trademark/copyright symbols in regular product copy:
- ✅ "Connect your Apple Pay account", "Link your PayPal account"
- ❌ "Connect your Apple Pay™ account", "Link your PayPal® account"
