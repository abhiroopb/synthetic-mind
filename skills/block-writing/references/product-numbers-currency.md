# Product Numbers: Currency and Quantities

## General Number Usage
- Use numerals (not words): "3 business days" not "three business days"
- Comma separators for numbers >3 figures: "$1,000" not "$1000"
- Abbreviate in limited space: 12K, 10M, 1B, 5T (not "10 thou", "27 mil")
- Spell out ordinals (first, second, third) — avoid "1st", "2nd", "3rd"
  - ✅ "Your first payment is due on Friday"
  - ❌ "This invoice will be sent on Nov 21st."

## Negative Numbers
Use en dash (–) for negative amounts; avoid parentheses:
- ✅ "Loan repayment    –$10.00"
- ❌ "Loan repayment    (-$10.00)"

## Communicating Quantities
Use ✕ symbol for quantities; blank space between elements:
- ✅ "2 ✕ hamburger", "Vanilla candle ✕ 1"
- ❌ "Hamburger (2)", "1 – Vanilla candle"
- Limited space: "Chocolate syrup ✕2" (omit space between numeral and ✕)

## Account Information

**Credit card numbers** (standalone): `[Card brand] [last 4 digits]`
- ✅ "Mastercard 7890", "Amex 7890"
- ❌ "MasterCard *7890", "AMEX …7890"

**Credit card in a sentence**: `[Card brand] ending in [last 4 digits]`
- ✅ "Your Visa ending in 4111 expires soon."

**Bank account numbers** (standalone):
- **Brand A**: `[Bank name] • [last 3 digits]` → "Chase •789"
- **Brand B**: `[Bank name] [last 3 digits]` → "Chase 789"

**Bank account in a sentence**: `[bank name] account ending in [last 3 digits]`
- ✅ "Your payment will be deposited in your Chase account ending in 789."

## Currency Formatting
- No space between symbol and number: "$42.89", "€240"
- Cents (<$1): use ¢ after the numeral → "25¢" not "$0.25"
- Whole dollar amounts: omit .00 for fees/subscription rates → "$29/month"
- Currency names (not amounts): lowercase → "dollars", "euros", "bitcoin"

## Processing and Transaction Fees
Use plus sign between percentage and fixed fee:
- ✅ "3.5% + 15¢"
- ❌ "3.5% + $0.15"
