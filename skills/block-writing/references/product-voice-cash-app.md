# Product Voice: Cash App

## Voice Characteristics
- **To-the-point**: Get straight to what matters
- **Approachable**: Conversational and friendly, not stuffy
- **Upfront**: Transparent, especially when things go wrong

## Target Audience
General consumers; wide age range (teens to older adults); varying financial literacy; personal finance focus

## Tone by Context

**Money movement** (send/receive/withdraw): Direct and efficient
- "Send money" not "Transfer funds"

**Account security** (verification, fraud alerts): Serious but not alarming; clear next steps
- "Verify your identity to keep your account secure"

**New features** (onboarding, discovery): Encouraging but not pushy; brief explanations
- "Get paid up to 2 days early with direct deposit"

**Errors**: Honest about what went wrong; solution-focused; never blame the user
- "We couldn't send your payment. Your card was declined. Try another card."

## Product-Specific Terminology

**Money movement**:
- ✅ "add money" (to Cash App balance), "send" (to someone), "withdraw" (to bank)
- ❌ "cash out" (deprecated), "transfer funds", "remit payment"

**Product names** (always capitalize correctly):
- Cash App, Cash App Card, Cash App Pay, Cash App Taxes, Cash App Investing

**Account identifier**:
- $cashtag (not "cash tag" or "CashTag") — "Your $cashtag is $alex"

**Features**:
- "direct deposit" (lowercase)
- "Cash Boost" (capitalize as specific product)
- "Bitcoin" not "BTC" in user-facing content

## Example Patterns

**Buttons**: "Send money", "Add to balance", "Request payment", "Withdraw to bank"

**Confirmations**: "Sent $50 to Alex", "Added $100 to your balance", "Withdrew $200 to Wells Fargo"

**Errors**:
- "We couldn't send your payment. Your card was declined. Try another card or add money to your Cash App balance."
- "This code expired. Request a new one."
