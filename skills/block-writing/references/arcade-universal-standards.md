# Arcade: Universal Content Standards

These guidelines apply to all Block products. Cash App/Arcade is the primary reference; adapt for Square, Afterpay, Tidal, Bitkey, and Proto where product-specific guidance doesn't exist.

## Casing
- Sentence case for ALL elements — capitalize first word and proper nouns only
- Product names maintain brand capitalization: Cash App Card, Square, Afterpay, Block

✅ "Save changes" / "Add payment method" / "Cash App notifications"
❌ "Save Changes" / "Add Payment Method"

## Punctuation
- **No end punctuation**: Labels, buttons, headings, short phrases, navigation
- **Use end punctuation**: Complete sentences, helper text, body text, descriptions
- **Questions**: Sparingly in labels; appropriate for modal confirmation titles
- **Exclamations**: Avoid in errors and alerts; use very sparingly elsewhere

✅ Button: "Save changes" | Helper text: "We'll send updates here." | Modal: "Delete account?"
❌ Button: "Save changes." | Helper text: "We'll send updates here"

## Voice and Perspective
- **You/your**: Second-person perspective throughout
- **Active voice**: Preferred across all products
- **Gender-neutral pronouns**: "their/they/them" — not "his/her"
- **Avoid articles**: Skip "a/the/this/your" unless they add clarity

✅ "Enter your email" | "Save your changes"
❌ "Enter the email address" | "Changes will be saved"

## Block Voice in Components
- **Crisp**: Keep labels brief; cut unnecessary words
- **Self-assured**: Direct, declarative language; no hedging
- **Fluent**: Adapt tone to component context and user state
- **Upfront**: Don't hide important information; be specific about outcomes
