# Product Mechanics: Punctuation

## Periods
- Headers, subheaders, buttons, labels: **no periods**
- Body copy, longer explanations: **use periods**
- Lists: use periods if items are complete sentences
- ✅ "Send money" (button) | ✅ "Your payment was successful. It should arrive in 1-3 business days."
- ❌ "Send money." (button with period)

## Commas
Use the Oxford comma (before "and" in lists of 3+):
- ✅ "You can send, receive, and spend money"
- ❌ "You can send, receive and spend money"

## Colons
Capitalize the first word after a colon:
- ✅ "Here's what you need: Your Social Security number and phone number"
- ❌ "Here's what you need: your Social Security number..."

## Semicolons
Avoid; use periods instead to keep writing conversational:
- ✅ "Your payment was sent. It should arrive soon."
- ❌ "Your payment was sent; it should arrive soon."

## Question marks
Use for direct questions only; no multiple punctuation marks:
- ✅ "Need help?", "What's your phone number?"
- ❌ "Need help?!", "What's your phone number??"

## Exclamation marks
Sparingly — major milestones only; never in error messages; never multiple:
- ✅ "You got paid!" | ❌ "You got paid!!!" | ❌ "Your payment failed!"

## Hyphens
Use in compound adjectives before a noun; not after:
- ✅ "Real-time notifications", "Two-factor authentication"
- ✅ "The notifications are real time" (after noun, no hyphen)
- ❌ "Real time notifications", "The notifications are real-time"

## Em dashes
No spaces around em dashes:
- ✅ "We sent your money—it should arrive soon"
- ❌ "We sent your money — it should arrive soon"

## En dashes
For ranges and negative numbers; no spaces:
- ✅ "Monday–Friday", "$5–10", "–$25"
- ❌ "Monday – Friday", "-$25"

## Parentheses
Use for additional info and optional field labels; avoid (s) for plurals:
- ✅ "Phone number (optional)", "Enter your SSN (123-45-6789)"
- ❌ "Add your bank account(s)"

## Slashes
No spaces around slashes; avoid as substitute for "or":
- ✅ "MM/DD/YYYY", "Enter your username or email"
- ❌ "MM / DD / YYYY", "Enter your username/email"

## Quotation marks
Don't quote UI elements; use bold instead:
- ✅ Tap **Send**, Go to **Settings**
- ❌ Tap "Send", Go to "Settings"

## Ampersands
Use "and" in body copy and buttons; OK in navigation where space is limited:
- ✅ "Terms and conditions", "Save and continue"
- ❌ "Terms & conditions" (body copy)

## Asterisks
Immediately after the referenced word, no space:
- ✅ "Get paid instantly*"
- ❌ "Get paid instantly *"
