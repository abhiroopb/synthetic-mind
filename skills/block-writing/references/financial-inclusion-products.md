## Brand B Borrow
- Short-term loans $20–$1,000; flat fee: $5 per $100 for 30 days
- Comparison: payday loans typically $30 per $100 for 14 days (~780% APR)
- Alternative data underwriting using Brand B transaction history — no traditional credit score required
- 97%+ repayment rate; cannot take new loan if current loan is overdue (prevents debt cycling)
- Use cases: 43% bill payment, 42% groceries/necessities, 38% cash flow smoothing
