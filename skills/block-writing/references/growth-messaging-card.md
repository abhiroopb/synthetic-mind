# Cash App Growth Messaging: Cash App Card and Features

## Cash App Card
Customizable Visa debit card; no hidden fees or minimum balance; unlocks banking features.
- ✅ "Cash App Card" — not "Cash Card"
- When meaning is clear, can just say "card"
- **Don't say "free card"** — most designs cost money now; don't use "Order a free Cash App Card"
- Don't say "prepaid debit card" externally (technically accurate but not how we refer to it)
- Don't say "everywhere/anywhere Visa is accepted" (compliance guidance—use "places where Visa is accepted")
- Virtual card + account/routing number included; works with Apple Pay and Google Pay
- Avoid absolutes about fees: some customers can get ATM fees if they don't have direct deposit

✅ "Order a Cash App Card" / "Design a Cash App Card" / "Cash App Card keeps getting better"
❌ "Order a free Cash App Card" / "Create a Cash App Card" / "Best debit card you can get"

**Disclosure**: "Prepaid debit cards issued by Sutton Bank, Member FDIC." (Use when mentioning Cash App Card or showing card image)

---

## Offers
Customers add offers to save instantly on purchases at specific stores/services. Not automatic—customers add one at a time.

- Customers "add an/the offer" — not "add an offer to a Cash App Card" (bank partners sensitive about this)
- Not cash back; it's **instant discounts**
- Don't capitalize "offers" (except in very rare "Cash App Offers" usage)
- "Add offer, use it. Add another. Repeat." — one offer at a time

✅ "Add the offer, then pay with your Cash App Card to save 10% at Finish Line"
❌ "Add the offer to your Cash App Card" / "Get cash back" / "Save anywhere" (store-specific)

**Disclosure**: "Offers provided by Cash App, a Block, Inc. brand."

---

## Exclusive Access
Cash App Card gives access to concert presales, special events, and more. Customers typically pay with or show their card.

- Don't imply which presales will be available or guarantee access
- Access is gated by having/using the card — not just having Cash App

✅ "Unlock exclusive access to concert presales, special events, and more with your Cash App Card"
❌ "As long as you have a Cash App Card, you'll get early access" / "If you have Cash App, you'll get in"

**Disclosure**: "Exclusive access provided by Cash App, a Block, Inc. brand."

---

## Round Ups
Rounds up Cash App Card purchases to the nearest dollar → stocks, bitcoin, or savings (one destination at a time).

- Always "**Round Ups**" — not "round ups", "round-ups", or "Round-ups"
- Customers "**turn on** Round Ups" — not "enable" or "activate"
- Use destination-specific verbs: "invest in stocks", "buy bitcoin", "add to savings"
- Don't say "invest" with bitcoin; don't imply multiple simultaneous destinations
- "spare change" and "nearest dollar" are standard descriptors

✅ "Turn on Round Ups to make your spare change go further"
✅ "Save, invest, or buy bitcoin automatically with your spare change"
❌ "Enable Round Ups" / "Save and invest with every purchase" (only one destination at a time)
❌ "Rounding up all of your transactions" (only applies to Cash App Card transactions)

**Disclosure**: "Round Ups provided by Cash App, a Block, Inc. brand."
