# Style: Capitalization

## Sentence Case (Default)
Use sentence case for all headings, buttons, navigation, labels, and UI elements.

**Rules**:
- Capitalize the first word only
- Capitalize proper nouns (Cash App, Square, names)
- Lowercase everything else

**Examples**:
- ✅ "Send money to friends"
- ✅ "Connect your bank account"
- ✅ "Cash App Card settings"
- ❌ "Send Money To Friends" (title case)
- ❌ "Connect Your Bank Account" (title case)

## Exceptions
- Product names: Always capitalize correctly (Cash App, Square, Afterpay, etc.)
- Proper nouns: Names, places, specific products
- Acronyms: ATM, PIN, ID (all caps)
