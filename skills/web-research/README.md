# Web research

> Search the web and synthesize external information into structured research briefs for product decisions.

## What it does

Generates diverse search queries from a research topic, searches the web, reads and extracts key content from the most promising sources, and synthesizes findings into a structured brief with categorized insights, source attribution, and actionable recommendations. Designed to complement PRD-writing and spec-creation workflows by providing competitor analysis, market trends, best practices, and technical context.

## Usage

Describe what you want to research and why. The skill handles query generation, search, extraction, and synthesis.

- "Research [topic] for a PRD"
- "What do competitors do for [feature]?"
- "Find best practices for [design pattern]"

## Examples

- `"Research how competitors handle price rounding at checkout"`
- `"What's the state of the art for offline payment processing in 2026?"`
- `"Find best practices for split payment UX — I'm writing a PRD"`

## Why it was created

Product decisions benefit from external context — what competitors do, what customers expect, and what industry trends suggest. This skill automates the research step that PMs typically do manually across dozens of browser tabs, producing a sourced, structured brief ready to feed into a PRD or spec.
